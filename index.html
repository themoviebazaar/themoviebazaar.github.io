How does

MovieBazaar Work?

Investors



General public can invest in movie ideas that they like,
which are presented on our platform in the form of
synopsis, trailers, shorts, etc

Producers



Once the budget requirement of a movie idea is fulfilled,
production will commence & it will be outsourced,
allowing us to produce multiple ideas simultaneously

Distributors



Movies produced will be distributed to OTT networks only.
The Profit Secured will be distributed back to the investors
proportionate to the investment made

The first of its kind

Decentralised Film Making Platform



Browse through a catalog of movie ideas on our website. Pick a movie of your liking & invest any amount in the movie's budget.
Once the budget is fulfilled, we make and sell the movie, in a way that it warns maximum profits & you will get a proportionate share from the profits made.
